# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful community for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project and users

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling or inflammatory language
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, open a private security report or contact maintainers through repository channels.
